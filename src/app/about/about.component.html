About Us

We keep you cool in the Summer, warm in the Winter, and secure all year round!

Founded in Stockton, CA in 2002, Mike’s Mobile has become Northern California’s premier home service company. Over the years, our dedicated team has garnered incredible experience while servicing thousands of homes. We’re committed to providing our customers with the highest quality services, care, and products possible, as well as keeping them comfortable in their homes!

Our Mission

Mike’s Mobile values our employees first. Then, as a team, we improve the homes of our customers and provide them with an exceptional experience that we would want for our own families.

To us, these words mean that our team relies on values, such as integrity and a desire to ensure customer satisfaction, in every interaction we have. We want our customers to feel supported and cared for from the moment they call us until the final touches are put on their homes!

Our Reach

Our service areas currently stretch from Yuba City to Fresno, covering Napa, San Jose, and the East Bay Area. Our main offices are located in Sacramento and Stockton, but all of our technicians are fully mobile and ready to come to your home regardless of where you fall in our service areas!

Our Qualifications

Your peace of mind and trust is of the utmost importance to us. This is why we ensure that all of our chimney and fireplace technicians are certified by the Chimney Safety Institute of America. We also prioritize ongoing education and training for all of our employees to make sure that our entire team is prepared to handle your needs and concerns.
