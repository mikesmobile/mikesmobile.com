We Fix Leaky Chimneys!

Protect your chimney from all angles with our 10-year warranty!

The last thing you want to worry about as a homeowner is water leaking into your home. That's why our waterproofing bundle includes all the repairs you need to ensure your chimney isn't the cause of any unfortunate leaks.

Our waterproofing package seals any leaks you may currently have in your chimney system and protects your chimney from water intrusion. Water intrusion is the leading cause of chimney degradation. Give your chimney a new lease on life, and protect your home.

The process starts with crown coat applied to your chimney crown to protect the integrity of the top of your chimney stack. Once the crown coat has cured, we install a chimney cap (not a flue cap!) to shed water away from the stack. We also address leaks at the roofline by applying flash seal where your roof meets your chimney to ensure a watertight seal. We finish our waterproofing package with a water repellent coating applied to the entire stack that allows the chimney to breathe and sweat while still repelling outside water.

The waterproofing package from Mike’s Mobile includes a 10-year leak free warranty and is 10% off with our bundled pricing.

A gas fireplace offers wonderful aesthetics and helps to keep your home warm during the cooler months. With regular maintenance, your system will be ready to go so that you can sit back, relax, and enjoy your gas fireplace!

Annual Maintenance for Gas Fireplaces

  • Even if you don’t notice any obvious issues, the National Fire Protection Association recommends an annual inspection to ensure that your gas fireplace continues to work properly.
  • Many homeowners' insurance policies require an annual professional chimney inspection and cleaning. Your insurance company may require that you provide proof of this when filing a claim.
